Engineering

Having studied Robotics I have a good knowledge of basic electronics, design of embedded systems and Artificial Intelligence. Photography

I always loved taking photos with my fathers’ camera and at the age of fourteen I got my first DSLR and what started out as a hobby has turned into freelance work. I love to photograph people in the moment and not in a big staged setup, which is how I started with event photography and then moved on to weddings. I started out with nature photography as this gave me more time to compose the pictures and I still carry my camera around with me to this day on trips and hikes.
